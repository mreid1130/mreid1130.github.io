About

Web Developer. Dev Bootcamp Student. Sports Fanatic.

I was born and raised in the Bay Area, educated at Occidental College in Los Angeles, CA, and have been working as an search engine optimization (SEO) specialist for UniversalGiving in San Francisco, CA since graduation. I thoroughly enjoyed my work at UniversalGiving and wouldn't trade the experience I had at that organization for anything. As an SEO specialist, I had to have basic working knowledge of HTML, so I decided to work through the CodeAcademy course for HTML and CSS. While going through that course, I realized how much I enjoyed building and problem solving in HTML, so I decided to branch out and try some of the other courses. After a few weeks of coding, I made my way through the Ruby, Python, and Javascript courses, but I didn't feel like I could use these languages in any practical way, so I felt a little stuck. I've always learned best when I totally immerse myself in a subject, and Dev Bootcamp is giving me the opportunity to do just that with web development.
