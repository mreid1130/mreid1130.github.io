Pair Programming 101

By Kevin Maze and Mark Reid

What is pair programming?

The best way to pair program is to think of it as a partnership. Each pair has a driver and a navigator. The driver tends to do most of the typing and the actual code work. The navigator directs the driver and focuses on the big picture.

Why is pair programming important to DBC??

Pair programming is important to DBC because it promotes teamwork, allows people to give and receive feedback, and gives additional perspective on how others program.

Compare Driver and Navigator

The driver essentially types the code and is the main medium between computer and output. The navigator is similar lays out a roadmap for the driver to follow, they create the map and tell the driver how to follow the map.
